# Master Research Canvas (RU/EN)

## 0) Objective

Validate product opportunity for an app combining gaming, astrology/esoterics,
avatar identity, coaching, and mindfulness.

## 1) Hypothesis v1

Users return daily when three mechanics are combined:

1. Personalized interpretation by birth date.
2. Avatar-based identity reinforcement ("best self").
3. Coaching/mindfulness prompts with visible progress.

Success signal: strong daily re-engagement intent and acceptable willingness to
pay versus adjacent category benchmarks.

## 2) Research Pipeline

1. Hypothesis framing.
2. Top apps scan (Top 200 x 5 categories).
3. Market validation (TAM/SAM/SOM + growth).
4. Global competitor analysis.
5. Whitespace synthesis.
6. Hypothesis revision (v2, v3...).
7. Go/No-Go decision.
