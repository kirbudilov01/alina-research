# Value Proposition v1 (RES-2)

## Core Promise

Alina gives the user a daily feeling of direction and momentum by combining
personal forecast, identity reinforcement through avatar, and short guided
actions for growth.

## Why This Can Win

1. Most apps solve one layer only: entertainment, spirituality, or productivity.
2. Alina combines emotional meaning and behavior change in one daily loop.
3. Avatar mechanic turns abstract self-improvement into visible identity.

## Value Pillars

1. Meaning: birth-date based interpretation creates personal context.
2. Identity: avatar reflects desired future self and progress.
3. Action: short coaching and mindfulness prompts convert intent into behavior.

## 30-Second User Pitch

"Open Alina each day, get your personal reading, see your best-self avatar,
and complete one short action to become that version in real life."

## Non-Goals (v1)

1. Full clinical mental health treatment positioning.
2. Hard deterministic promises or unverifiable claims.
3. Heavy social network scope in MVP.
