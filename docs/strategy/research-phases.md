# Research Phases

## Phase 1: Hypothesis Baseline

- Lock hypothesis v1.
- Define validation and falsification criteria.

## Phase 2: Top-200 App Scan

- Collect top 200 apps per niche.
- Normalize core fields and tag features.

## Phase 3: Market Validation

- Estimate TAM/SAM/SOM.
- Assess growth signals and market momentum.

## Phase 4: Global Competitive Intelligence

- Build competitor landscape and positioning map.
- Analyze pricing, retention, and content strategies.

## Phase 5: Whitespace Synthesis

- Identify under-served intersections.
- Generate updated hypotheses.

## Phase 6: Decision Pack

- Produce go/no-go memo.
- Define next research iteration roadmap.
