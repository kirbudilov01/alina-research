# Niche Scope Matrix (RES-3)

## Scope Rule

Each niche must contribute at least one retention mechanic and one monetization
hypothesis. If not, it stays secondary for MVP.

## 1) Gaming

- In scope: progression, streaks, rewards, collectible identity elements.
- Out of scope: complex PvP systems, long-session gameplay.
- KPI expectation: D1/D7 return uplift via loop design.

## 2) Astrology/Esoterics

- In scope: daily interpretation, symbolic narrative, reflective prompts.
- Out of scope: absolute future guarantees and high-risk claims.
- KPI expectation: daily open intent and emotional engagement.

## 3) Avatars/Identity

- In scope: photo/video avatar, style evolution, progress-linked visuals.
- Out of scope: AAA 3D pipeline in MVP.
- KPI expectation: attachment and re-open motivation.

## 4) Coaching

- In scope: micro-goals, check-ins, nudges, reflection loops.
- Out of scope: licensed therapy positioning.
- KPI expectation: action completion and streak continuity.

## 5) Mindfulness

- In scope: short breathing/focus rituals, reset flows.
- Out of scope: long premium content library at launch.
- KPI expectation: session completion and stress-relief self-report.

## Intersection Hypothesis

Highest potential intersection for MVP:
Astrology context -> Avatar identity cue -> Coaching action -> Mindfulness reset.
