# Validation and Falsification Criteria (RES-6)

## Decision Framework

Hypothesis is valid only if demand, retention logic, and monetization potential
are supported by external evidence and competitor benchmarks.

## Validation Criteria

1. Demand Fit
- Top-200 scans show repeated demand for each core component.
- At least two strong overlap clusters across niches.

2. Behavioral Fit
- Existing apps prove daily loop viability for similar mechanics.
- Candidate loop supports low-friction completion in under 10 minutes.

3. Commercial Fit
- Comparable products show acceptable monetization patterns.
- At least one feasible pricing model for MVP.

4. Strategic Fit
- Clear whitespace with weak direct substitutes.
- Differentiation can be explained in under one sentence.

## Falsification Criteria

1. Component overlap is weak and artificial.
2. Retention relies on high-content cost not viable for MVP.
3. Monetization evidence is inconsistent across adjacent categories.
4. Compliance risk is high with no realistic mitigation path.
