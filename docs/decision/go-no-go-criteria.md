# Go/No-Go Criteria

## Go Signals

1. Clear demand evidence for at least 3 of 5 pillars in same target segment.
2. Defensible whitespace at intersection level.
3. Viable unit economics assumptions.
4. Practical compliance path.

## No-Go Signals

1. Fragmented demand with weak overlap.
2. Saturated market without credible differentiation.
3. Unsustainable monetization model.
4. High legal or trust risk without mitigation.

## Decision Inputs Required

1. Hypothesis revision history.
2. Top-200 synthesis across categories.
3. Market sizing assumptions.
4. Competitor matrix and positioning.
5. Risk register.
