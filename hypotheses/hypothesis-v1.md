# Hypothesis v1

## Product Core

Alina is a daily companion app combining:

1. Birth-date based interpretation and forecast.
2. Avatar identity (photo/video) representing the user's best self.
3. Daily coaching + mindfulness actions.

## Target User (Initial)

Young to mid-age mobile users who already consume self-improvement, spiritual,
and gamified products and want identity reinforcement in daily life.

## Core Jobs

1. Feel direction and emotional certainty for today.
2. See a motivating version of self and stay consistent.
3. Receive short, actionable daily guidance.

## Validation Signals

1. Users understand the value proposition in less than 30 seconds.
2. Users express intent for daily usage.
3. Evidence from top apps confirms partial demand for each component.

## Falsification Signals

1. Weak overlap between segments across five niches.
2. No strong retention mechanics in comparable products.
3. Poor willingness to pay for combined proposition.

## Next Revision Trigger

Revise to v2 after Top-200 scan and feature intersection analysis.
